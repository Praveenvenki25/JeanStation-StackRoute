About Me

JeanStation is a fast fashion e-commerce company. The company mainly focuses on men's jeans, but it also offers women's apparel and children's clothes JeanStation is currently in Canada but our next target to open our store in Europe, America, Australia, and the Middle East along with other consumer markets. We belives that fashion is part of life.

The team travel globally to source the best new brands and have a love for denim. This fabric is highly regarded due to the traditional process of weaving the denim on the original shuttle looms. Work on these looms is a slower process, producing small quantities of fabric, but it creates an imperfect and varied denim that lends a character not found on modern machines. Likewise, the artisanal techniques used in the dying process produce a unique indigo finish. These traditional techniques create the characteristic fades and creases which are unique to each wearer and much sought after by denim fans.

The Jean Store staff are denim enthusiasts, which gives them not only an in-depth knowledge of the processes behind denim making, but also of how to care for your denim after purchase. They understand how specific brands will fit and variations in brands sizing. Jean Store’s focus is to source the best craftsmanship and most interesting denim brands for their customer.
